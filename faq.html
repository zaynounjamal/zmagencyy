Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does ZM-Agency offer?

ZM-Agency offers a wide range of digital services including web design and development, digital marketing, SEO, social media management, and mobile app development.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

The timeline for a website project can vary depending on its complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a more complex e-commerce site could take 8-12 weeks or more.

Do you offer ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. Contact us for more details on our maintenance plans.

What is your approach to SEO?

Our SEO approach combines on-page optimization, quality content creation, technical SEO, and ethical link-building strategies. We tailor our approach to each client's specific needs and industry.

Can you help with social media management?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive social media management services, including content creation, scheduling, community engagement, and performance analysis across various platforms.

What platforms do you use for e-commerce websites?

We work with several e-commerce platforms, including Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento. We'll recommend the best platform based on your specific needs and goals.

Do you offer custom mobile app development?

Yes, we provide custom mobile app development services for both iOS and Android platforms. We can also develop cross-platform apps using technologies like React Native.

How do you measure the success of digital marketing campaigns?

We use various analytics tools to track key performance indicators (KPIs) such as website traffic, conversion rates, engagement metrics, and ROI. We provide regular reports and insights to our clients.
